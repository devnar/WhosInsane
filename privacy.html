Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how personal information is collected and used during the use of the Discord bot provided by Who's Insane.

What information do we collect?

Who's Insane collects only the minimum information necessary to save your Discord server and manage game sessions. This may include server IDs, channel IDs, and game session data.

How do we use your information?

We use the information we collect solely to provide the functionality of Who's Insane in your Discord server. We do not share or sell this information to third parties.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If any changes are made, it is recommended that you review this page to keep track of the changes.
